Email-Based Pet Reminder System

DigiPet provides an email-based reminder system that helps users keep track of important pet care tasks such as feeding, grooming, vet appointments, and medication schedules. Reminders are sent directly to the user’s email, ensuring notifications are received on time even when the user is not logged into the website.

Pet Profile Management

The website allows users to create and manage individual pet profiles by entering their pet’s name. Each pet profile stores reminders specific to that pet, and users can access these reminders by clicking on the pet’s profile, making it easier to manage multiple pets efficiently.

User Blog Feature

DigiPet includes a blog feature that enables users to upload and share articles or information related to their pets. This feature allows users to share experiences, tips, or educational content, helping to build a small community and increase user engagement on the website.
